## Planetary Transits & Their Meanings

### Mercury Retrograde
Duration: Approximately 3 weeks, 3-4 times per year
Effects: Communication issues, technology problems, misunderstandings, travel delays
Advice: Review, revise, reconsider. Not ideal for starting new projects.

### Venus Retrograde
Duration: Approximately 6 weeks, every 18 months
Effects: Relationship challenges, financial reviews, reassessing values
Advice: Good time for introspection in love and finances. Avoid major commitments.

### Mars Retrograde
Duration: Approximately 3 months, every 2 years
Effects: Energy levels lower, projects may stall, conflict resolution needed
Advice: Complete unfinished business. Avoid aggressive confrontations.

### Jupiter Retrograde
Duration: Approximately 4 months, annually
Effects: Expansion slows, reflection on growth and beliefs
Advice: Internal expansion. Review opportunities. Not ideal for expansion activities.

### Saturn Retrograde
Duration: Approximately 5 months, annually
Effects: Review of responsibilities, past patterns resurface
Advice: Address karmic lessons. Work on discipline and structure.
